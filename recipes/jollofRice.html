Jollof Rice

Of course, this list of popular Nigerian foods would not be complete without the inclusion of jollof rice. Regarded as the staple of West Africa, it’s Nigeria’s go to meal for every party, celebration, event, hang out, etc. You name it, jollof rice is there! It’s enjoyed by many other countries ranging from Ghana to Sierra-Loene, but each country does have its own unique variation of the meal. For here in Nigeria, ours is known to be spicy and often highlights ingredients like peppers and crayfish.
